Learning Plan

What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

I have been working in the tech industry for almost 5 years now juggling different roles under the hat. I have always wanted to contribute to something by actually building it or a part of it, so I have decided to follow my passion and pursue a career in software development. There are a lot of pros of working in the tech industry which I’m living, however, the best part is that you can work from anywhere i.e. flexibility, which I love the most.


A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning:

My strengths are that I’ve an always learning attitude, and a result it will keep me motivated on my career pathway of becoming a software developer. One of my limitations is that, I sometimes doubt my abilities to perform a task. This is something that I have been working on by taking up on difficult tasks and giving them a shot.


What human skills would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to strengthen my learning mindset and would like to boost my confidence in my skills and expertise. Also, I would like to encourage myself in reaching out to the wider community when struggling to find something as that would help me get to the solution or path to it efficiently.


A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

One thing that I do and works best for me is planning the whole week in advance and set daily goals. It does take a little bit time of your rest day, however, I have found it extremely useful. There will times where you couldn’t reach your goal but you’ll definitely find a way to do it the next day. Also, using the time box technique with regular breaks in between each task really helps you achieve your results efficiently.


A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way:

The first place to seach for an issue would be check our Dev Academy server as this would save a lot of time trying to google and going through different websites. In case, there is nothing in discord, I’ll use google for help. After trying my luck on google, say about 30 minutes, I’ll come back to discord and post my question in the relevant channel.


A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

As a student, I would expect my facilitator to be friendly, knowledgeable and always there to support me in this journey. I would also expect the facilitation team to provide constructive feedback through out the course as this helps one understand what they are doing good and where’s the room for improvement.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I’m fully committed to the course and I have complete support from my partner.
